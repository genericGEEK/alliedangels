Allied Angels Programs

ALL Haven Gathering Monthly

Join us monthly on the first Saturday at 10 AM at Sunrise shopping center. These open meetings are a cornerstone of our community. It's a space for peer survivors to connect, share experiences, celebrate progress, and offer mutual support. Enjoy snacks, walk together, and find encouragement as you navigate your path to healing by walking and talking. ALL survivors are welcome no matter what step of your journey you are on.

ALL Haven Flight Club Weekly

For survivors ready to deeply engage in their healing, our structured program offers personalized support. Participants set meaningful goals rooted in their trauma stories and healing journeys. As they achieve each goal, they earn their wings, symbolizing their progress and resilience. This program is facilitated through our weekly goal meetings.

Our Voices, Our Change CampaignJoin Us

True support and prevention require a shift in collective understanding. We actively work to bring together current resources, educate systems, and communities about the pervasive issues of violence and abuse. Through compelling YouTube videos, podcasts, events, emails, and infographics of collective data from survivors stories. We share powerful survivor testimonials and insights. These voices serve as crucial tools to illuminate the realities on the ground, inform current systems about necessary changes, foster safer communities, and empower women experiencing trauma from violent acts.
