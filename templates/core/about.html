Recently asked questions

People Are Frequently Asking Some Questions

Questions are welcome here. We believe clarity builds safety, and understanding helps you decide what feels right for you as you explore our community.

How do I sign up?

You can start by sharing your contact information through our Connect page and letting us know what you’re interested in. We’ll follow up with next steps when you’re ready.


You’re also welcome to attend any of our scheduled events. You do not need to sign up in advance to attend an event. Visit our Events page to see upcoming times and locations.

How much does it cost?

There are no membership fees. You can donate if you have funds for that, but it is not necessary for attendance or participation.

What is the difference between the ALL Haven Gathering and the Flight Club?

The gathering happens once a month and is a time to meet new people, walk, talk, and get used to people in the group on a regular basis and in a more casual environment. The Flight Club weekly meetings are when you sign up for the All Haven Goal Program. You will get a program manual. Journal and discuss difficulties accomplishing your goals. Allied Angels will help you plan your SMART goals and provide resources on how to accomplish each of your goals, we will celebrate your progress, and earn your wings and in the end your halo to begin mentoring others working on their goals.
